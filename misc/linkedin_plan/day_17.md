# Day 17: Platform Engineering vs. SysAdmin (Strategy Pillar)
**Hook:** A SysAdmin builds a server. A Platform Engineer builds the *way* to build a server.

As I transition from Systems Engineering to Platform Engineering, the biggest shift hasn't been the tools—it's been the mindset. 

At a national bank, we're moving toward:
- **Self-Service:** Enabling devs to provision environments without waiting on a ticket.
- **Paved Paths:** Standardizing Docker workflows for high-compliance environments.
- **Observability by Default:** Building monitoring into the infrastructure from day zero.

Platform Engineering is about reducing cognitive load for developers while maintaining the strict guardrails of enterprise banking.

**CTA:** Is your team moving toward a "Platform" model, or are you still in traditional Ops?
