# Day 18: Cold Outreach with a Brain (AI/Automation Pillar)
**Hook:** Can you run a cold email marketing system for $0 and still have a 10% conversion rate?

For a project in the industrial automation niche, I didn't want a complex CRM. I wanted a lean, intelligent system.

The "Zero-Budget" Stack:
1. **Google Sheets:** To manage the lead list.
2. **Claude API:** To analyze the lead's business and draft a *hyper-personalized* intro.
3. **Brevo:** To handle the delivery via their free tier.

By using AI to bridge the gap between "Bulk Email" and "Personal Connection," we built a lead gen machine that actually respects the recipient's time.

**CTA:** Personalization at scale: Is it the future of marketing, or just better spam?
