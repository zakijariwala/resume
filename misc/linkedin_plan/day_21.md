# Day 21: Sunday Personal: The ECE to SRE Journey (Personal Pillar)
**Hook:** I started in Electronics & Communication (ECE), but I found my home in Systems Engineering.

People often ask why I moved from hardware and circuits to high-availability banking infrastructure. 

The truth? They aren't that different. 
- In ECE, you learn about signal integrity and low-level protocols. 
- In SRE, you learn about system integrity and high-level orchestration. 

Both require the same discipline: Understanding the "Failure Modes" before you build the "Success Path." Whether it’s an ESP32 or a Tier-1 Bank IDM, the principles of reliable engineering remain the same.

**CTA:** What was your "starting point" in tech, and how has it shaped your current career?
