# Day 22: Prediction: AI in Incident Response (Strategy Pillar)
**Hook:** In two years, the first person to respond to your production incident won't be a human. It will be an AI Agent.

We're moving beyond "Auto-restarting" services to "Auto-diagnosing" them. 

Imagine an SRE Agent that:
1. **Correlates:** Real-time logs with recent GitHub commits across 150+ servers.
2. **Predicts:** Identifying an RTO risk before the system actually fails.
3. **Drafts:** Preparing a "Resolution Report" before the incident is even closed.

At a national bank, we're already seeing how automation reduces human error. AI is just the next layer of the shield.

**CTA:** Is AI the future of SRE, or is it a security risk we aren't ready for?
