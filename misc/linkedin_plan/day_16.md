# Day 16: Humanizing AI Prose (AI/Automation Pillar)
**Hook:** AI writing is easy. Making it sound like a human who actually knows what they're talking about is hard.

In my **Medium Article Automation** project, I quickly realized that raw LLM output is too generic for technical readers. 

To fix this, I engineered the **"Failure First" Humanization Framework**:
- **Strict Heuristics:** Enforcing rules to eliminate "AI-isms" (e.g., "In the ever-evolving landscape...").
- **Context Injection:** Feeding the model real project constraints and "failure stories."
- **Heuristic Validation:** A secondary layer that "kills darlings" and ensures publication-ready quality.

The result isn't just "content"—it's a technical narrative that adds value.
