# Day 19: Managing the Hybrid Fleet (SRE/Ops Pillar)
**Hook:** Managing 150+ RHEL and Windows servers is like conducting an orchestra—if one person is out of tune, the whole show is ruined.

Supporting a Tier-1 financial institution means balancing the stability of Windows with the flexibility of Linux (RHEL). 

The key to keeping this fleet healthy? **Radical Standardization.**
- **Patching:** Automated cycles that treat servers like cattle, not pets.
- **Access:** Centrally managed via IDM workflows.
- **Incident Response:** Standardized runbooks that apply to both OS environments.

When you scale infrastructure, complexity is the enemy. Consistency is your only shield.

**CTA:** Linux vs. Windows in the enterprise: Do you prefer a monoculture or a hybrid fleet?
