# Day 24: 50+ Coaches later: The Interview Gap (Growth Pillar)
**Hook:** I’ve coached 50+ candidates for technical and management roles. The biggest gap isn't technical skill—it’s **Situational Awareness.**

In high-stakes interviews (like for Tier-1 Banks), candidates often answer the question they *wanted* to be asked, not the one that was actually asked. 

The "Audit-Ready" Interview Mindset:
- **Precision:** Don't generalize. Use metrics (e.g., "50% RTO reduction").
- **Constraint Awareness:** Explain *why* you chose a specific path given the regulatory or budget limits.
- **Leadership Narrative:** Show how you mentored others during the project.

**CTA:** What is the hardest technical interview question you’ve ever faced?
