# Day 20: Technical Storytelling (Growth Pillar)
**Hook:** Your code might be a 10, but if your explanation is a 2, the business sees a 0.

During my coaching sessions for 50+ engineers, I’ve found that the best technical leaders are actually great storytellers. 

They don't just present a diagram; they tell a story of:
1. **The Conflict:** What was the mission-critical bottleneck?
2. **The Choice:** Why did we pick X over Y under these constraints?
3. **The Resolution:** What was the impact on uptime, cost, or user experience?

If you want a seat at the table, you have to learn to speak the language of "Value," not just "Variables."

**CTA:** How do you translate your technical wins for non-technical leadership?
