# Day 25: Security vs. Agility (Strategy Pillar)
**Hook:** In banking, "Move fast and break things" is a great way to lose your license.

The daily battle of an SRE in a financial institution is balancing strict regulatory compliance with the need for modern developer speed. 

How we bridge the gap:
- **Security-as-Code:** Building ACLs and firewall rules into the CI/CD pipeline.
- **Compliance Guardrails:** Pre-hardened Docker images that are audit-ready out of the box.
- **Automated Governance:** Ensuring access is granted through policy, not manual tickets.

Agility isn't about ignoring the rules; it's about automating the proof that you followed them.

**CTA:** Security or Speed? Which one usually wins in your organization?
