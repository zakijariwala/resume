# Day 28: Weekend Recap: My Top 3 Technical Failures (Growth Pillar)
**Hook:** Success is just a collection of failures you decided to learn from.

As I look back on my time leading Ops for a national bank, these were my "Best Mistakes":
1. **The "Manual Overwrite" Mistake:** Trusting my memory instead of the runbook (Result: A 30-min delay).
2. **The "Silent Fail" Mistake:** Building a script that didn't alert on failure (Result: A weekend spent manual patching).
3. **The "Too Much Automation" Mistake:** Automating a broken process before fixing it.

Failures are the only way to build a "Failure First" mindset. 

**CTA:** What is the most valuable mistake you’ve ever made in production?
