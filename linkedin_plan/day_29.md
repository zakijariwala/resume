# Day 29: Posting Daily on LinkedIn: The Result (Personal Pillar)
**Hook:** I’ve posted every day for 29 days. Here is what I learned about the "Developer Personal Brand."

Most engineers think LinkedIn is just for recruiters. It’s not. It’s for:
- **Refining your thoughts:** Writing daily forced me to articulate *why* I do what I do.
- **Building a Network:** I’ve connected with more SREs and AI Engineers in 30 days than in the last 2 years.
- **Creating Opportunities:** Visibility attracts what you actually want to work on.

Consistency is a superpower. Whether it’s code commits or content, the compounding interest is real.

**CTA:** Are you a "Lurker" or a "Creator" on LinkedIn? What’s stopping you from sharing?
