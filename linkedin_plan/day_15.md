# Day 15: The "Why" behind Identity & Access Management (SRE/Ops Pillar)
**Hook:** In a bank with 30,000+ users, Identity isn't just a login—it's the perimeter.

Supporting a mission-critical Identity & Access Management (IDM) platform for a leading financial institution has taught me that Security and Operations are two sides of the same coin. 

When you manage access at this scale:
1. **Automation is survival:** You can't manage 30k identities manually.
2. **Audit trails are everything:** Every permission change must be traceable.
3. **Governance is the goal:** Moving from "who has access" to "why do they have access."

IDM is the foundation of modern Zero Trust architecture, and in enterprise banking, it's where the battle for stability is won.
